Why Music?

Einstein once said: “Life without playing music is inconceivable to me. I live my daydreams in music. I see my life in terms of music... I get most joy in life out of music”.

Music is all around us. Music is in movies, TV shows, videos, etc. Without music life would be bland.

Wouldn't it be cool if you could play some of your favorite themes from shows or movies that you love?

Learning an instrument provides a healthy creative outlet and can be a form of therapy.

This beginner piano and scales course can help you learn the basics.

Most mobile piano apps will work. If you do want to use a computer, this free website also has a piano to use.
